ABOUT US

Established in 2014, Kencana Shop is a store that sells several various types of Muslim women's favourite clothing, such as Abaya, Baby Doll, Hijab, House Dress, Bath Towel, and Sarong. The owner of this shop is Dewi Kencanawati, this shop does not have employees so she is the owner and the person who runs this business. This store serves online and offline sales, Kencana Shop has an offline store located at Jalan Parang Kusumo 3 no 3, Sondakan, Laweyan, Surakarta which operates every day from 07:00 AM to 07:00 PM. then this store also provides online transaction services, the payment method is bank transfer or e-wallet and the products will be sent via courier and delivery services.

Our collections are comfortable, stylish, and cheap. Our clothing is made up best materials that last and last. We want our customers to feel confident and beautiful in today’s society, and we believe they should not have to sacrifice style because of some good fashion products with high prices out there.
